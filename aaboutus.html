About Us

Welcome to our golf resort, where we blend world-class golfing with exceptional service and a relaxing atmosphere. Our resort offers stunning views, luxurious accommodations, and a welcoming environment for golf enthusiasts of all levels. Whether you are here for a round of golf, a stay at our luxurious resort, or to enjoy our dining options, we aim to provide an unforgettable experience.

Founded in 2015, our mission is to deliver excellence and create lasting memories for all of our guests. Managed by Troon Golf, we pride ourselves on exceptional course conditions and the highest standard of service. Experience the best of golfing and luxury with us.
